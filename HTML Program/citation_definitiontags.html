Brihadishvara Temple (originally known as Peruvudaiyar Kovil) locally known as Thanjai Periya Kovil, and also called Rajarajeswaram, is a Hindu temple

Chola, who commissioned the temple, called it Rajarajeshwaram (Rajarājeśwaram), literally the temple of the almighty of Rajaraja.
